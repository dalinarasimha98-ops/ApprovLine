# Business Continuity Plan

Core continuity order is authentication, tenant database access, approval evidence, audit logs, exports, integrations, queue processing and AI enrichment. If Redis or AI is unavailable, disable queue-dependent actions and show operational warnings while read-only evidence access continues. If an integration is unavailable, preserve its cursor and retry after provider recovery. Maintain two authorized operators for Vercel, Supabase, Clerk, Redis, Sentry and DNS. Review contacts and recovery access quarterly.

Customer support uses the status page and incident templates. Security/legal review any data-loss or breach notification obligations. A quarterly tabletop exercise and a semiannual technical restore test are required.
