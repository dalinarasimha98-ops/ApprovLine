How it works

From scattered messages
to a searchable record.

Approline runs invisibly in the background. No new tools for your team. No forms to fill. It reads, classifies, and stores — so you have everything when it matters.

⚡

Connect in 15 minutes

OAuth connections to Gmail, Slack, and Teams. Read-only access — Approline never writes or modifies anything in your tools.

⬡

AI classifies every message

Built on Claude. Detects explicit approvals, implicit sign-offs, conditional approvals, and escalations — with a confidence score on every record.

≡

Unified decision timeline

Every captured approval lands in a searchable, filterable dashboard. One source of truth across every tool your organization uses.

↓

Audit-ready exports

Export your full decision log as PDF or CSV, formatted for compliance and legal review — with timestamps, evidence links, and approver details.

⊕

SOC 2 from day one

Enterprise-grade security. AES-256 encryption, TLS 1.3, tenant data isolation, and a clear path to SOC 2 Type II and GDPR compliance.

Find anything in seconds

Full-text search by keyword, person, date, source, or topic. What used to take 3 weeks now takes 3 minutes.
