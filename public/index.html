Decisions happen everywhere.
Records exist nowhere.

Modern teams use 8+ tools simultaneously. Every critical approval disappears into threads, inboxes, and call recordings, until something goes wrong.

CFO approves in Slack

Budget increase agreed in a thread, then buried under 500 messages.

Legal signs off over email

Contract terms confirmed in an email chain nobody can find six months later.

Product gets a thumbs-up on Zoom

Verbal go-ahead on a call. No record. No proof. No trail.

Auditor asks: who approved this?

3 weeks of manual reconstruction. Fines. Disputes. Damaged trust.

The gap ApprovLine fills: No tool today captures approvals passively, across all tools, with AI classification, and presents them as a unified auditable timeline.
How It Works

Set up once. Capture everything.

ApprovLine works invisibly in the background, no new habits, no forms to fill, no behavior change required from your team.

01

Connect your tools

Link Gmail, Slack, Teams, and more in under 15 minutes. Read-only OAuth, so we never write or modify your messages.

02

AI classifies approvals

Every message is analyzed by our AI engine. Explicit, implicit, and conditional approvals are detected with a confidence score.

03

Decision records created

Each approval becomes a structured record: who, what, when, where, evidence, and a link back to the original thread.

04

Search and export anytime

Real-time unified dashboard with full-text search. Export your complete audit log as PDF or CSV in seconds.
